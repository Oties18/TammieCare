Comprehensive Pediatric Care

At TammieCare, we offer a wide range of pediatric services to ensure your child's well-being from infancy to adolescence.

Routine Check-ups

Regular health assessments to monitor your child's growth, development, and overall health.

Immunizations

Providing vaccinations to protect your child against serious diseases and illnesses.

Newborn Care

Specialized care and guidance for your newborn's health, including feeding, sleeping, and development.

Our Key Services

Well-Baby Visits

Our well-baby visits ensure your infant is thriving and developing properly. We provide thorough physical exams, growth measurements, and offer advice on feeding, sleep, and safety.

Developmental Screenings

We conduct regular screenings to evaluate your child's physical, emotional, and cognitive development. Early detection of potential delays allows us to intervene and provide the necessary support.

Acute Illness Treatment

We offer prompt diagnosis and treatment for common childhood illnesses, including ear infections, fevers, respiratory infections, and gastrointestinal issues.

Adolescent Care

We provide specialized care for teens, addressing puberty, mental health, and preventive care to ensure a smooth transition to adulthood.

Emergency & Urgent Care

We provide swift care for urgent situations to ensure your child's safety and well-being.

24/7 Urgent Care

We're available around the clock for medical emergencies, offering fast diagnosis and treatment for injuries and acute illnesses.

Injury Care

Our pediatricians are trained to handle common childhood injuries such as fractures, cuts, and bruises with care and expertise.

Fever & Illness

If your child is suffering from a sudden high fever, severe cold, or flu, we provide immediate care to manage symptoms and prevent complications.

Specialized Pediatric Services

Allergy & Asthma Care

We provide comprehensive management plans for children with allergies and asthma, helping them breathe easier and avoid triggers.

Nutrition & Weight Management

Our team offers dietary counseling and nutrition plans to help children maintain a healthy weight and develop lifelong healthy eating habits.
